Lobster

6 servings

2 hours

Ingredients

  • Lobster tails
  • Salt and peper
  • Butter
  • Thyme

Directions

Gather the ingredients.

With sharp kitchen shears it is best to cut down the middle of the back towards the lobster tail. The shell side will be up and the lobster tail will be pointed away from you.

Flip the tail over and carefully pull apart the middle of the cut shell and gently place your fingers underneath the lobster meat.

Flip the lobster back over, slowly and carefully pull up the lobster meat in one piece. The meat should still be attached at the bottom of the tail.

Then, you'll remove the mixture from the heat and cool it to room temperature.

Enjoy!
